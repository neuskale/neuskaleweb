Two Owner Search: When and Why You Need One

📅 June 20, 2023 ⏱ 5 min read 👤 Neuskale Team

Service Overview

Understanding the specific requirements and benefits of different title search services helps you make informed decisions about which search type best fits your transaction needs. Each service type is designed to address specific due diligence requirements at different price points and turnaround times.

The title search industry offers a range of services from basic ownership verification to comprehensive 30+ year chain of title research. Selecting the appropriate search type depends on the transaction type, lender requirements, and the level of risk the parties are willing to accept.

What This Search Includes

Each search type includes specific components designed to address particular aspects of property title verification. Understanding what's included helps you set appropriate expectations and ensures you're ordering the right level of service for your needs.

  • Ownership Verification: Confirming current and/or historical ownership through recorded deed documents.
  • Lien Detection: Identifying mortgages, tax liens, judgment liens, and other claims against the property.
  • Encumbrance Review: Examining easements, restrictions, and covenants that may affect property use.
  • Document Analysis: Reviewing the completeness and accuracy of recorded documents in the chain of title.

When to Use This Service

Different transaction scenarios call for different levels of title search detail. Purchase transactions typically require more comprehensive searches than refinances, while commercial transactions may have additional requirements beyond standard residential searches.

Working with an experienced title search provider helps ensure you order the right service type. At Neuskale, our team can recommend the appropriate search based on your specific transaction details, saving you time and money.

Turnaround and Delivery

Turnaround time is a critical factor in title search ordering. Delays in receiving search results can hold up closings and create downstream issues for all parties involved. At Neuskale, we prioritize fast turnaround without compromising accuracy.
